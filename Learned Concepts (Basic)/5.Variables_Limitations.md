# Variable Declaration
- 📜There should ***no signs and no symbols and no space(gap)*** in variable 
---
- Examples:
 - 🌟int a,b,c ...✅
 - ⭐int a1,a2,a3 ...✅
 - 💥int net ...✅
 - 🔹int 1a,2,a* ...❌
 - 🔥int net sal ...❌
 - ✨int netsal ...✅
 # int Variable ***Limiation***
- 🔹a=10000 ...✅
- 🔸a=40000 ...❌
> ***int variable,MAXIMUM allowed value is '-32768 to 32767'***.
